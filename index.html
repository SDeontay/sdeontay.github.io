Who I Am?

I'm Deontay Stroud, a multi-talented graphic designer from Miami, Florida. I have been doing graphic design work with a bit of photographyfor 2 years now. I attended F.I.R.S.T. Institue located in Maitland, FL and finishing with a diploma in Graphic Design & Web Development in MARCH 2022. I'm looking to join a company were can I can continue to learn and grow within the graphic design field. .

What I Do?

I specailize in the adobe creative suite creating eye-catching logos, cover arts, motion graphics (logos, flyers, and cover arts), and ads. The suites that I am most comfortable in are Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects but that does stop me from learning more and perfecting my skills as a graphic designer.
